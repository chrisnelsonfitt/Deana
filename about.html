About

My name is Deana Johnson. Lover of coffee (with cream), sunshine, banjo music, good books, hammocks, and daily adventures.

I'm a photographer on a mission to find some of the most crazy, beautiful moments in the world around me. I guess you could say I'm in love with joy, laughter, and "true love". I live in Kearney, Nebraska, but am always itching to get out of town and go explore some place new or make some new friends.

I believe that photos can capture a memory like none other and tell a story that everyone can understand. I am passionate about people and love that I get the honor to be invited into some of the most magical, special moments of love in peoples' lives.

Thank you for sharing your life on camera for me to capture.

Thanks for taking the time to check out my page!


Much love,
-Deana
